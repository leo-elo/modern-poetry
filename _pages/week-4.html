Thursday, September 12

In class

  • Discussion of Amy Lowell's poetry
  • Lecture and discussion on the end of Imagism and introduction to Vorticism.

Assignment

  • Read Vorticism in The Princeton Encyclopedia of Poetry and Poetics, at least as far as the section on Modern Poetry.
  • Explore and read Blast.
  • Read the rest of "A Retrospect" by Ezra Pound (pgs. 429-433 in our textbook or here).
  • Read chapter on Ezra Pound and her poems in our textbook up to but not including The Cantos.
  • Write a forum post in which you discuss a poem by Ezra Pound and how it exemplifies his poetics. Post in our forum before class on Tuesday.
